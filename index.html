AI Prisoner's Dilemma

You are playing the Prisoner's Dilemma game with an AI agent. You have two choices: "Cooperate" or "Defect". There are four possible outcomes:
  • If you choose "Cooperate", and the AI agent does too, you'll both get +60 score.
  • If you choose "Cooperate", and the AI agent chooses "Defect", you get -50 score, and the AI agent gets +145 score.
  • Similarly, if you choose "Defect", and the AI agent chooses "Cooperate", you get +145 score, and the AI agent gets -50 score.
  • If you choose "Defect", and the AI agent does too, you'll both get +10 score.
This game will run for 10 rounds. The grids below will update based on the AI's decision - green meaning "Cooperate" and red meaning "Defect". Yours and the AI's total score as the game progresses will also be shown below.
